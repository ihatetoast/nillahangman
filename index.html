Bird, Beast, or Fish

How to play

  • You will be given the name of an animal with only the first and last letters shown.
  • You will be told whether the animal is a bird, beast, or fish. "Beast" will include land mammals, reptiles, insects, and spiders; "Fish" will include true fish as well as other water-dwelling animals (eg. lobster, octopus, whale).
  • If the animal's name consists of two or more words, only the initial of the first word and the last letter of the last word will be shown (eg. bald eagle: B - - - / - - - - E).
  • You have seconds to guess the animal.
  • If you guess correctly, your score increases by the number of intermediate blanks.
