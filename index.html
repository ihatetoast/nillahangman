Bird, Beast, or Fish

How to play

You will be given the name of an animal with only the first and last letters shown. Your only other hint will be whether the animal is a bird, beast, or fish. In this game, "Beast" will cover mammals, reptiles, arthropods, and "Fish" will cover true fish as well as other water-dwelling animals. If the animal's name consists of 2 or more words, only the initial of the first word and the last letter of the last word will be shown (eg. bald eagle: B - - - / - - - - E).

You have seconds to guess the animal. If you are successful, your score increases by the number of intermediate blanks. If you are not, the computer gets that number. First to 100 wins
